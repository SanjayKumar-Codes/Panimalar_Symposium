Bitblitz

Coding Contest

Join us for an exhilarating Coding event at Panimalar Engineering College's Technical Symposium. Unleash your coding prowess and innovative spirit as you tackle exciting challenges and compete against fellow coding enthusiasts. Show off your skills, solve complex problems, and showcase your creativity in this thrilling competition. Don't miss this opportunity to demonstrate your coding expertise.

Round 1:

Team Size: Each team comprises 2 members.

Programming Language: Python.

Systems will be provided; no need to bring laptops.

No electronic gadgets allowed.

Strict prohibition on switching tabs during coding; any detected will lead to disqualification.

Time Limit: 45 minutes.



Round 2:

Top 5 teams selected for Round 2, based on points scored in Round 1.

All Round 1 instructions apply to Round 2.

Time Limit: 30 minutes.
